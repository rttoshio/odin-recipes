Spicy Carrot

These tender and roasted carrots cooked in the air fryer can be on your table in less than half an hour. Tossed in a honey-butter sauce and sprinkled with your choice of fresh basil, chives, or just salt and pepper.

Ingredients
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted
  • 1 pound baby carrots
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon hot honey
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed orange juice

STEPS

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Combine butter, honey and the carrots in a bowl. Mix it and transfer the carrots to the air fryer basket
  3. Air fry until carrots are roasted and fork tender, tossing every 7 minutes, for 15 to 22 minutes. Mix orange juice with reserved honey-butter sauce. Toss with carrots until well combined. Season with salt and pepper.
